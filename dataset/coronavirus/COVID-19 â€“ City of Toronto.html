Protect our communities, and yourself. Everyone is now required to wear a face mask or face covering when entering indoor public spaces and travelling on the TTC.

Current Health Situation

As of 3 p.m. on July 27, 15,338 cases of COVID-19 in Toronto have been reported to Toronto Public Health and 13,785 people have recovered. More details on the status of COVID-19 cases in Toronto, including active outbreaks in Toronto long-term care and retirement homes and shelters, are updated three times per week on Monday, Wednesday and Friday. Read the class order issued by Toronto’s Medical Officer of Health on April 1.
